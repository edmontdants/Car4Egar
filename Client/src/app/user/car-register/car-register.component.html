Register Car Form

Here are some tips for car registration.
1 - The data must be completely correct and in conformity with reality, in order for the renter to know the actual specifications of the car.
2 - If the car was insured by the competent authorities, this must also be true, so that you send us these documents at the time of the request.
3 - You can only register the car at the present time and make it not currently rentable and change that at a later time.
4 - You can easily upload a picture of the car after registering it by logging into your profile.
